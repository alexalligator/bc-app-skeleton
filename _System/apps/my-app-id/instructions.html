Klarna Integration - Version 1.0

Description

Klarna payment integration for Business Catalyst

Requirements

  • A klarna account with merchant ID and shared secret credentials
  • Credentials provided to FunBit and saved on integration server

App Settings

Take note of the following settings used by this app:

  • Country: Norway (NO)
  • Language: Norwegian-Bokmål (NB)
  • Locale: Norwegian Bokmål, Norway (NB_NO)
  • Currency: Norwegian Krone (NOK)
  • Tax Rate: 25 %
  • Max one discount code per order

Instructions

Terminology

When a customer makes a purchase on your store using Klarna a reservation is created.

This Klarna reservation is saved with the order in your Business Catalyst CRM database

When you are ready to ship, you activate the reservation

Klarna send the customer an invoice and send you your money. The original reservation is deleted.

Managing Klarna orders

Log in to your Klarna online account to view reservations and invoices

Use this app to cancel, activate and refund orders

Important!This app does not make changes to the orders in your CRM database. It only operates on your Klarna Online reservations and invoices.
